Orgee Solar lighting systems

People at Orgee computers are experts consultants when it comes to your lighting needs. Our solar powered lighting system has already lighted many outdoors, lawns and houses. Orgee solar panels generate and store electricity in a battery. At dawn, the LED lights are lit automatically and draw electricity from the battery.

LED lights have a advantage of very low power consumption and much brighter light compared to traditional bulbs/tubelights and CFLs. This helps in longer hours of lighting at absolutely no cost.

Our most common solar panel setup constists of the follow components:

  • Solar panels: 2 x 40W panels
  • Pole: 16 ft in length
  • LED lights: 2 sets of 6W/850 lumens lights
  • Battery: 12V/80Ah Orgee battery

The above mentioned specifications are just for reference. Actually specifications change based on customers requirement and setup.
